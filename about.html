Hi there. I'm Chris Clarke and I design software products for some reason.

I started illustrating as mr mr in the early 2000's, making random and thoughful flyers for the Numbers club night in Glasgow. All while I was college learning something called web design. I moved to London in 2006 and got stuck there ever since.

I've been making a mess in design for a decade now, in various guises, working in media, agency and Q&A setups. I work for the Guardian desiging their apps and video products.

I've worked for the Guardian, Yahoo!, General Assembly and Poke. Clients include: Nike, Skype, Diesel, Ted Baker, EE and Orange. I'm on all the usual social media haunts, though I'm more active desiging and doodling really ;)
